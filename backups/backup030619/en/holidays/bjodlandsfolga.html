Bjødlandsfolgå was moved to the farm only a few years ago, but the original timber walls are probably around two hundred years old. The house was moved to the farm and rebuilt just a few years ago. Here it has been renovated.

It has 4 beds in 2 bed rooms. Like the other houses, it has electricity, hot and cold water, modern kitchen and bathroom.
