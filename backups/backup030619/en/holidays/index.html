Fossane is not just another holiday accommodation. Fossane is your holiday.

What we offer is peace and silence, activity, and fun, all at once. Meet the people, animals and the nature of the farm.

"No TV" is also a gift from us to you, as we often say it (although if you really need it, you can borrow one). The water you drink on the farm comes from our own ground. The water has been given excellent reports by analysts. The electricity you'll use is produced by our own "green" hydroelectric powerplant. We'll be more than happy to you show you around.

Fossane makes your holiday, and your holiday makes Fossane. Welcome!
