Welcome to Fossane  
Fossane is not just another holiday accommodation. Fossane is your holiday.

What we offer is peace and silence, activity, and fun, all at once. Meet the people, play with the animals and enjoy the nature around the farm.

"No TV" is also a gift from us to you, as we often put it (although if you really need it, you can borrow one). Our drinking water comes from our own well and has been given excellent reports by analysts. The electricity you'll use is produced by our own "green" hydroelectric powerplant. We'll be more than happy to you show you around.

Fossane makes your holiday, and your holiday makes Fossane. Welcome!
