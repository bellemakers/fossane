How to find us

Booking
The farm Fossane is located in Hjelmeland kommune (council of Hjelmeland) on the south-west coast of Norway, about 60 km nort-east of the city of Stavanger. The nearest town center is Hjelmeland.

Vormedalen, the village where you find Fossane, is about 20 km from Hjelmeland, and here you can find a sign to Fossane.

The following link sends you to a map on the Norwegian Yellow Pages (Gule Sider® at www.gulesider.no). Fossane is marked by a red flag. Click on this to zoom in. You can also go to "Driving directions" to get the shortest driving route from any location in Norway. Choose Fossane as your "stop location", and enter the address or name (after pressing "more options"). Also see some of the examples below.
