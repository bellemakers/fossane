 Il bagno  
In summer time, you can go swimming in the rivers and lakes around the farm. You don't need artificially built water slides and massage showers, it is all provided by the nature.

Slide on a thick layer of moss in the river, or feel the force of 20 litres of water per second on your shoulders. The water that supplies a special natural pond, has run in a very shallow stream for kilometres, and thus been heated by the sun, reaching temperatures of up to 30°C on a sunny day. The lakes are also suitable for a longer swim. People of all ages, including toddlers, can find something that suit their skill level.

If you prefer ocean waters, why not visit the fjord beach only 4 km from the farm, or the nice beach area at Hjelmeland. The beaches close to Stavanger are also well known internationally for their surf. Bring your wet suit and surf board!
