Independent of who you are, you'll find something to do while at Fossane. You can of course choose to relax in a couch with a good book, but you've also got the opportunity to take part in other activities on the farm.

You can help run the farm, fish in rivers, lakes or in the ocean, go swimming or go for a walk in our beautiful nature. Use the icons above to find out more.

The city centres of Stavanger and Sandnes are just one and a half hour away with a car if you want more urban activities.
