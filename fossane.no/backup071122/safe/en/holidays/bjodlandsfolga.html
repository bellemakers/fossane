 Bjødlandsfolgå  
Bjødlandsfolgå was moved to the farm only a few years ago, but the original timber walls are probably around two hundred years old. Here it has been renovated.

It has 6 beds in 2 bed rooms (see interior pictures below). Like the other houses, it has electricity, hot and cold water, modern kitchen and bathroom.
