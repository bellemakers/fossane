The nature invites to hiking for everyone 
The nature provides a beautiful and varied landscape in which it is inspiring to walk. The mountains dominate the horizon, but this does not necessarily mean that all walks are strenuous. You can choose to walk along the gravel road or an easy path in the forest. Do you still want a different challenge, the mountains are free for anyone to explore.

The famous mountain formation of the Pulpit Rock (Preikestolen in Norwegian) is visited by tourists from all over the world. It is a plateau of about 30 x 30 meter jutting out of the steep cliffside, 600 meters above the famouse fjord Lysefjorden. The feeling one gets from looking at the boats below can't be described with words, it has to be experienced!

The starting point for the walk to Preikestolen lies on hours drive from the farm. The walk itself takes about 2 hours one way, so all in all it is a suitable day trip while you're staying at the farm.

Another exciting excursion is the walk to Skomakarnibbå, our local version of the Pulpit Rock. Enjoy the view of the entire Ryfylke region while walking on top of the 700 metre tall cliffs. This trip does not suit small childreN!

Feel free to ask your landlady or landlord about where to go. They both have years of experience in the mountains, and know what might suit your preferences. They can also provide maps.
