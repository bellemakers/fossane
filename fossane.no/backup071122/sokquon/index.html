Sotto Voce - by Sokquon Tran

Sotto Voce (beneath the voice) is a collection of charcoal drawings on paper completed early this year.

The artist's statement

The images are a reflection on our subconsciousness which have a strong reference to our lives. The dynamics which exist in these images, are a result of the subconscious mind, when it is in the state of dreaming and is in the process of embracing an uncertain reality. The beauty of being in this state, is that one is uncertain about one's destiny. I believe it is the finest state due to the fact that ideas and concepts can be transformed. Beginnings and ends become irrelevant. What is relevant is that we are trying to measure these uncertainties. One step towards determining these qualities, is to explore.

The images

The actual images measure approximately 650 x 940 mm. They are under glass and have an ivory mat board with dark pacific maple as frames. The size of the framed image is appr. 920 x 1200 mm.
