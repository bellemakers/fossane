 Folgå  
[The following text is only in English] Folgå is the oldest house on the farm, and was probably built in two stages. The northernmost part is the oldest part, but when a bigger house was needed in the 19th century, it was expanded by adding another living room and a bed room.

It has 9 beds in totally 4 bedrooms. Like the other houses, it has electricity, hot and cold water, modern kitchen and bathroom.
