Three houses on the farm are renovated and used as holiday houses  
Would you like to have your holidays on our farm? Here is something for everyone, whether you would like a peaceful retreat or a holiday full of activities! Although all of the three houses are antique, they all have electricity, hot and cold water, and modern bathrooms and kitchens. The houses have been renovated during the last 10 years, but the old timber walls, the original furniture and the original atmosphere are all still there.

You can rent any of the three houses on the farm at any time of the year. The price for each of them is the same, but they vary slightly in size, so take your time to browse through the descriptions and pictures of each of them. Follow the links to the left to learn more about each house.
