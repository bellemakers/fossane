 How to find us  
The farm Fossane is located in Hjelmeland kommune (council/municipality of Hjelmeland) on the south-west coast of Norway, about 60 km nort-east of the city of Stavanger. The nearest town centre is Hjelmelandsvågen.

Vormedalen, the village where you find Fossane, is about 20 km from Hjelmeland, and here you can find a sign to Fossane.
