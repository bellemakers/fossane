You're welcome to help run the farm!

You can participate in the daily work at the farm, or just observe us. At Fossane we've got sheep, geese, ducks and a friendly cat.

Between June and September/October most of the sheep are in the mountains, but some are usually still on the farm. Several guests have had a great experience when helping to gather the sheep back from the mountains in autumn. Around April or May the sheep give birth, so this is also a very exciting time.

Feel free to bring your own pet, from abroad or from other parts of Norway, but please make sure you're following the correct legal guidelines.
