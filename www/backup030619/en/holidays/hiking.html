How to find us
Booking
The nature provides a beautiful and varied landscape in which it is inspiring to walk.

The mountains dominate the horizon, but this does not necessarily mean that all walks are strenuous. You can choose to walk along the gravel road or an easy path in the forest. Do you still want a different challenge, the mountains are free for anyone to explore.

Feel free to ask your landlady or landlord about where to go. They both have years of experience in the mountains, and know exactly what might suit your needs. They can also provide maps.
