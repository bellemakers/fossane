Jonsenhuset ("Jonsen's house") is the "newest" of the three houses. It was built about 100 years ago, and moved to the farm in the 30s.

It has 8 beds in totally 3 bedrooms. Like the other houses, it has electricity, hot and cold water, modern kitchen and bathroom.
